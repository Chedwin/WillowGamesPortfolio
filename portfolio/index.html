About




Hello!


I'm a computer programmer with 3+ years of (game) programming experience. Currently located in Toronto, ON. Open to relocation within Canada and the USA.


Graduate of Humber College Game Programming, Class of 2017.


On the technical side, I focus mainly on programming core gameplay mechanics and primary functionalities of games. I am also extremely interested in game engine design as it allows total control for the programmers to utilize system hardware and resources.


My game design philosophy is to create every user-interactive game element to be as intuitive as possible. I believe that the easier it is for a player to catch on to a game's mechanics, the better experience it will create for the player to continue playing.


I thrive in creative and collaborative environments where hard work and passion for making great games is valued, and where I can leverage my strong technical background to enhance my designs and team.
